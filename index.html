Apartments
Comfort and Style. Make yourself at home.
From the second to the sixth floor, apartments of various
sizes from 35 to 80 m2 are harmoniously planned, the
residents of each of which are guaranteed maximum privacy
and comfort.
Penthouses
A lifestyle, inside and out.
There are several penthouses on the seventh floor of the
building. At the disposal of their residents: high ceilings of 5.5
meters, free layout to create a stylish, solemn interior.
True luxury should be
elegant, cozy, original.
Open eco-terrace in the Eastern part of the house,
quiet and protected from the city noise, for those who
prefer to have a Cup of coffee at dawn in summer
under the first rays of the sun, or those who decide to
soak up the sun by noon. Your evening will pass
unnoticed for cozy conversations in the fresh air, and
in winter the terrace will turn into its own ice rink for
residents and their guests.
